Anjanapura Sri Raghavendra Swamy Temple Trust

ABOUT us

The Anjanapura Sri Raghavendra Swamy Temple Trust is a devout organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the legacy of Sri Raghavendra Swamy, a revered saint in the Madhvacharya tradition. Our temple serves as a spiritual sanctuary for devotees seeking blessings, knowledge, and inner peace.

Our Mission

  • To maintain a sacred space dedicated to the worship of Sri Raghavendra Swamy.
  • To provide a welcoming environment for all devotees, regardless of background or belief.
  • To promote the teachings and philosophy of Sri Raghavendra Swamy through daily prayers, festivals, and educational programs.
  • To foster a sense of community and social responsibility among our devotees.

    • Our Values

  • Devotion: We are dedicated to serving Sri Raghavendra Swamy with utmost devotion and reverence.
  • Inclusivity: Our temple welcomes everyone seeking spiritual growth and connection with the divine.
  • Education: We strive to educate devotees about the life and teachings of Sri Raghavendra Swamy.
  • Community: We foster a sense of community and belonging among our devotees.
  • Service: We believe in giving back to society through charitable works inspired by Sri Raghavendra Swamy's message.

    • Get Involved

    The Sri Raghavendra Swamy Temple Trust thrives on the contributions of our devotees. There are many ways you can get involved:

  • Volunteering: Donate your time and skills to help with temple operations, festivals, or community events.
  • Donating: Your financial contributions allow us to maintain the temple, conduct pujas, and offer educational programs.
  • Spreading the Word: Help us reach more people by sharing information about the temple and our activities with your friends and family.

    • We invite you to visit our temple and experience the spiritual peace it offers. Become part of our growing community and embark on a journey of devotion and self-discovery with the blessings of Sri Raghavendra Swamy.
